Who am I?
Sanjay Singhal.

Sanjay, a proud Canadian, Tech Entrepreneur and author, is best known for founding and selling the renowned Audiobooks.com.

He is President of the Aquanta group, a Partner for 500 Startups Canada, as well as an AngelOne investor. Sanjay has successfully co- founded over 6 companies, while being an ongoing investor in over 20 startups.

He is a long standing supporter of SANSAR - the South Asian Network Supporting Awareness & Research. A non-profit organization that is committed to the awareness and promotion of cardiovascular health for the South Asian population through research, education and clinical initiatives.

His Memoir “Zero to Tesla,” published in 2018 chronicles the whole improbable journey and how failure doesn’t prevent success, it only delays it.

These day’s Sanjay works on perfecting the service industry with the opening of his first Lounge in Toronto ON, where he develops new technologies that help elevate the customer experience. A combination of systems and training work to improve and individualize the experience of each person that walks through the door. From food and beverage to greetings and goodbyes, Coffee Oysters Champagne aims to achieve a more personal and higher level of customer service that can be applied to all areas of the hospitality industry.
